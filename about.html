  My background

Born in 1973 in North Macedonia, I completed the vocational school for ICT in 1992 and a Postgraduate Diploma in Information Systems Management in 2019. I have a solid background in IT Solution Architecture in multiple industries (7 years in Utilities, 3 years in Banking, 20+ years in Telco Business Support Systems).
My native language is Macedonian; I speak a couple of other Slavic languages, have excellent command of spoken and written English, and rudimentary (but improving) Spanish.

I currently live and work in Dubai, UAE.


  What do I do

As a Principal Solution Architect, I partner with the account management teams to identify and create opportunities. I drive the value argumentation and technical proposal creation, and I assume leading architecture roles in presales and execution – from inception to handover.
During those engagements, I work closely with the customer C-Level executives, their Enterprise and Solution Architects. I identify the target Architecture Vision, design the business and technology transformation blueprint, and create appropriate architecture roadmaps tailored to the business vision of the customer.
Finally, I work with the project teams as a lead architect to create the system solution design, and I guide the technical project execution until the rollout.
